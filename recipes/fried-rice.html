Description

Fried Rice is a classic comfort dish made with cooked rice stir-fried with garlic, eggs, vegetables, and savory seasonings. It’s flavorful, filling, and a great way to turn leftover rice into a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1–2 eggs
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, optional)
  • 1–2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant and slightly soft.
  3. Push the aromatics to one side and scramble the eggs in the pan.
  4. Add the cooked rice and break up any clumps.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and stir well.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, then season with salt and pepper.
  7. Stir-fry for a few minutes until everything is evenly mixed and heated through.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
